About This Project

The Campus Lost & Found System is a simple, student-driven initiative created as a part of our Design Thinking project. Our goal is to help students reclaim their lost belongings faster and reduce campus confusion.

We built this platform using basic HTML & CSS to ensure it is easy, accessible, and maintainable by any student in the future.
